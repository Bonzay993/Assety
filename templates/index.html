Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for all new users.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no extra fees.

What types of assets can I manage?

You can manage hardware, software, licenses, cloud resources, and peripheral devices from a centralized dashboard.

Is the system cloud-based?

Yes, our IT asset management system is fully cloud-based, allowing you to access your assets from anywhere with secure authentication.

Does it support integrations with other tools?

Absolutely. We support integrations with major platforms like Microsoft Intune, ServiceNow, Jira, and more.

How secure is my data?

We implement enterprise-grade security protocols, including encryption at rest and in transit, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits.

Can I assign assets to specific users or departments?

Yes, assets can be assigned to individual users, departments, or even locations for better tracking and accountability.

Is there a mobile app available?

No, but we are planninh to offer a companion mobile app for both Android and iOS to manage assets on the go.
